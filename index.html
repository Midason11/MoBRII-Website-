About

MoBRII is dedicated to advancing molecular biosciences, enzyme engineering, and biotechnology innovation. Our work focuses on translating fundamental biochemical research into practical solutions.

Research Areas

Glucose Oxidase Engineering

Overexpression, purification, and optimization of glucose oxidase for industrial and biomedical applications.

Biosensor Development

Immobilization of enzymes on nanomaterials for glucose sensing and diagnostic technologies.

Bioremediation

Application of biological systems to detoxify oil-contaminated environments and restore ecosystems.
