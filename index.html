About the Researcher

MoBRII is a molecular biosciences research initiative focused on enzyme engineering, biosensor development, and environmental biotechnology.

The founder is a biochemistry researcher working on glucose oxidase overexpression, protein purification, immobilization on nanomaterials, and biosensor design.

Research interests include enzyme kinetics, protein chemistry, bioremediation, and diagnostic system development.

Research Areas

Glucose Oxidase Engineering

Overexpression, purification, and optimization of glucose oxidase for biosensor applications.

Biosensors

Development of enzyme-based biosensors using nanomaterials for glucose detection.

Bioremediation

Biological solutions for oil pollution and environmental restoration.
